Game Instructions

Objective: Your task is to navigate the snake, collect blocks, and avoid bombs and self-collision. Each block you collect or shoot scores points. But be careful, as each block collected makes the snake grow longer, making the game increasingly challenging!

Controls:

  • Right Arrow: Turn the snake right.
  • Left Arrow: Turn the snake left.
  • Up Arrow: Turn the snake up.
  • Down Arrow: Turn the snake down.
  • X key: Shoot bullets if you have any in reserve.

Game Elements:

  • Snake: Your character. It moves around the board and grows each time it collects a block.
  • Blocks: Collect or shoot these to score points.
  • Bombs: Avoid these! Contact with a bomb will cost a life.
  • Bullets: You can shoot these after collecting a certain number of blocks. Use them to remove blocks from a distance.

Scoring and Snake Growth:

  • Shooting a block with a bullet: 15 points, and the snake does not grow.
  • Collecting a block by the snake: 10 points, and the snake grows.

Game Rules:

  • The game ends when you lose all lives.
  • Collision with the snake itself or with a bomb will result in a loss of life.
  • After losing a life, the snake resets to its initial length.
  • The game continues as long as you have at least one life remaining.

Tips:

  • Plan your moves to avoid traps.
  • Use bullets strategically to remove hard-to-reach blocks.
